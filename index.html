About me


Hi, I am Jiaxi Yang. I am a senior majoring in Computer Science at University of Southern California. I transferred from University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign, where I spent two years studying CS.

I was a Software Engineering Intern at Meta on the End-to-End Encryption team, which powers the encryption component for the audio and video call of Messenger and Facebook family of apps.

In 2021, I interned at TikTok. I supported the backend of TikTok Ads data platform Libra, an A/B Testing web application.

I am proficient at Java, C++, Python, C, Swift, SQL, HTML, CSS, JavaScript, PHP. My interests lie in Full-stack Web Development. I have experience in Mobile Development of both Android and iOS Apps.
